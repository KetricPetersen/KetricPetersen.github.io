Speed Racer

Speed is a determined young driver who’s constantly pushing himself to be the best on the track. He drives the Mach 5, a high-performance car designed by his father, and races not just to win, but to prove himself and do what’s right. Speed is courageous, quick-thinking, and always ready to stand up for what he believes in even when the stakes are high.

Pops Racer

Pops is Speed’s father and the genius behind the Mach 5. He’s a former racing industry engineer who left to build cars on his own terms. Though he’s tough and sometimes short-tempered, Pops is deeply devoted to his family and works tirelessly to support Speed’s dreams while keeping him grounded.

Spritle & Chim Chim

Spritle and Chim Chim are the comic relief of the Racer family—but they’re more than just troublemakers. Spritle, Speed’s younger brother, and his chimpanzee sidekick Chim Chim often sneak into the Mach 5 or get caught up in the action. Despite their mischief, they’re genuinely clever and sometimes end up helping in surprising ways.

Trixie

Trixie is Speed’s girlfriend and a skilled pilot who plays an essential role in his racing adventures. Whether she’s flying overhead in her helicopter or helping solve problems on the ground, she’s smart, brave, and incredibly dependable. Trixie brings energy and insight to the team and she’s never afraid to speak her mind.

Racer X

Racer X is a mysterious, masked driver with a hidden past, he’s actually Rex Racer, Speed’s older brother, who left home years ago. Now working undercover, he uses his racing skills to take down corruption and protect Speed from afar. His character adds a deeper emotional layer to the story, as he silently supports his brother while hiding his true identity.
